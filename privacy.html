Privacy Policy

Piotr Jamróz built ’afterwriting as an Open Source application and provides it at no cost under MIT license.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decided to use the application.

Information Collection and Use

’afterwriting is a front-end only application. It does not collect any personal information and does not store user data in any database, however a user can decide to save a screenplay to 3rd party providers, e.g. Dropbox or Google Drive.

The application uses Google Analytics services that may collect information used to identify you.

Link to privacy policy of third party service providers used by the application:

  • Google Analytics and Google Drive
  • Dropbox

This policy is effective as of 11 September 2020.
